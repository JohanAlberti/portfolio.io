Self Evaluation

The ISS training has greatly enriched my skills. Indeed, being a student in Engineering Physics, the ISS training mixing students from Automation and Electronics and Computer Science and Network allowed me to acquire skills in software development, hardware development through many interdisciplinary projects.

I was also able to deepen the knowledge I already had within themes mixing IoT and societal issues and was able to address ethical issues within humanities courses.

In addition, I was able to enrich my knowledge on the Internet of Things (IoT). The numerous courses offered were very detailed on this topic and I appreciated the in-depth study of innovative protocols just on the market.

I believe that throughout my training I have acquired skills that complement my engineering degree in engineering physics and that will be useful in my future job.

However, some of these skills have yet to be fully acquired, in particular the one with a more IT-related aspect.

To conclude, this analysis of competences through a self-assessment and a portfolio is interesting to see the work done throughout the year and provides an interesting showcase for future applications.
